NAO - Out of the box¶

  1. Open the box!

    If this is your first time handling NAO, read the Safety Guide:

    Make sure you mind your fingers!

    Warning

    avoid carrying and touching NAO when moving, walking or getting up, you could be severely pinched.

    NAO‘s joints can be extremely strong.

    Warning

    More specifically:

    do not place your hands in any of its joints, as you could be severely pinched.

  1. Unpack the robot...

    Follow the steps of the Hello Guide.

  2. Then start the configuration trough the NAO - Getting started wizard.
