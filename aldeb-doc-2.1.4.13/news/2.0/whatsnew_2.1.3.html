Release Notes - 2.1.3¶

Continuous diagnosis notification fixed¶

Fix #29013: Flood of diagnosis notifications¶

In some cases, when ShoulderPitch motor temperature rises over 69 degrees, a continuous flow of diagnosis warning notification had an unwanted effect: NAO beeped continuously, while his Chest button flashed.

For further details, see: Diagnosis - NAO - notifications.
