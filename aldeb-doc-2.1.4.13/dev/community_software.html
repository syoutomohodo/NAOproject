Retrieving software¶

Warning

You do not have an Aldebaran robot yet?

You will not be able to download any software except Choregraphe trial version.

To download software from Aldebaran Community website:

Step Action

Make sure your NAO is registered on your Aldebaran Community user account.

For further details, see: Registering your NAO.

Access the software resources of Aldebaran Community website:

https://community.aldebaran.com/en/resources/software

Sign in, using your Aldebaran Community user account credentials.

All software available are displayed.
