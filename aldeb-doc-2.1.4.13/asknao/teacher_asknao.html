Teacher home¶

The Teacher home shares the Child tab with Parents.

Additionally, the Teacher home displays the: Playlist tab, Play tab, and Create tab.

Customizing Child Tab¶

The Child Tab displays condensed profiles of the children who already have accounts created. Children have to be registered through the administration account.

Please provide a list of children names and birthdays to your ASK NAO Administrator and request accounts created under your profile.

To find out who is the ASK NAO Administrator:

  • Click the Add a new child button:

    The error message below displays the names and emails of all the ASK NAO Administrator.
