How to access to the ASK NAO Interface - V2¶

To log into ASK NAO Interface - V2:

Step Action

Click the link: https://interface2.asknao.com/

The Login or create an account page appears.

I cannot click the link:

  • Open a web browser (Internet Explorer, Chrome, Mozilla, Safari, etc).
  • In the address bar, type: interface2.asknao.com
  • Hit the Enter key.

Type your Email and Password.

Use the password you received by email.

Click the Sign On button.

First login only

if a message “Asknao is requesting permission to do the following”,

Click the Allow button.

The home page appears, according to your profile:

  • Parent home
  • Teacher home
  • Administrator home

Tip:

  • You may create a bookmark or add to your favourite to keep the address.
